Who We Are

BellView Healthcare is India’s new rural-first pharmaceutical backbone, reshaping medicine access from the grassroots up. Headquartered in West Bengal, we’ve scaled to 330+ retail stores and robust in-house manufacturing—making affordable, high-quality medicines available across pan-India.

By integrating wholesale, ERP-driven supply chains, and digital-first distribution, BellView bridges India’s rural-urban health gap. Our founder, Aishi Singh Dastidar, brings over 12 years’ hospital leadership to a bold vision: accessible care for every Indian, through world-class pharmacy logistics and technology.

When to Seek Emergency Care

  • Chest pain or difficulty breathing
  • Severe allergic reactions (swelling, difficulty breathing)
  • Major trauma, burns, or severe injuries
  • Signs of stroke or heart attack (sudden weakness, slurred speech, chest pain)
  • Profuse bleeding or deep wounds
  • Loss of consciousness or unresponsiveness
  • Severe abdominal pain
  • High fever with confusion or seizures
